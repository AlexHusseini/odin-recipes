Alfredo Sauce

Alfredo sauce is a creamy, rich white sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. It's smooth, cheesy, and slightly garlicky, perfect for coating pasta like fettuccine. It's a popular comfort food and can also be used on pizza, in casseroles, or as a dip.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • fresh parsley
