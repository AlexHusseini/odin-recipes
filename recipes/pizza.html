Pesto Pizza

Home

Pesto pizza is a flavorful twist on classic pizza, featuring a vibrant basil pesto sauce instead of tomato sauce. Topped with mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and sometimes grilled chicken or ricotta, it's baked until golden and bubbly. Perfect for a fresh, herby, and cheesy bite!

Ingredients

  • 12 inch pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 ounce can chopped black olives, drained
  • 1/2 small red onion, chopped
  • ounce can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
